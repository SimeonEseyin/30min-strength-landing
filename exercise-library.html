Exercise Library

Use this page as a quick reference for the main movement buckets inside the program. For the full coaching notes and exercise-specific cues, open the exercise detail view inside the app.

Best use: Find the movement pattern you need, then review the matching in-app exercise card before you train. The app remains the source of truth for the exact exercise assigned to your current day.

Lower Body

  • Goblet squat and split squat patterns for leg strength and control.
  • Romanian deadlift and hinge work for glutes, hamstrings, and posture.
  • Lunge and step-up variations for unilateral balance and knee stability.

Push

  • Floor press and bench-style pressing patterns for chest and triceps.
  • Overhead pressing variations for shoulder strength and stability.
  • Push-up patterns where bodyweight options fit the plan best.

Pull

  • One-arm rows and chest-supported rows for upper back strength.
  • Rear-delt and scapular stability work to support posture and shoulder health.
  • Grip-focused pulling work to reinforce load control at home.

Core and Carry

  • Plank, dead bug, and anti-rotation work for trunk stability.
  • Loaded carries and bracing drills to tie full-body strength together.
  • Simple finishers that build work capacity without dragging workouts out.

How to Use It

  • Open your assigned day in the app first.
  • Tap any exercise for the detailed coaching notes and demo links.
  • Use the timer and feedback flow to log whether the load was too easy, solid, or too hard.
  • If a movement bothers you, reduce load first and use the exercise detail view before substituting anything.

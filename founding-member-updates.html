Founding Member Updates

This page is the running snapshot of what is shipping, what just changed, and what the current product focus is. It is meant to keep early members oriented without turning the app into a changelog wall.

Current Focus

  • Making reminders and push notifications reliable across devices.
  • Improving plan defaults and quiz-to-app setup quality.
  • Tightening the in-app experience so progress survives refreshes and device changes.

Recent Product Changes

April 2026

Reminder scheduling and push delivery were tightened so saved reminder changes correctly reset daily send state.

April 2026

Behavior-based reminder logic was added for first workout, incomplete session, inactivity, and weekly catch-up nudges.

April 2026

The in-app setup and quiz seeding flow were refined so defaults are imported once instead of constantly overriding user choices.

How to Send Feedback

If something feels broken, unclear, or slower than it should be, send the issue and device/browser details to devdad.desk@gmail.com. Tight bug reports are more useful than general frustration.
